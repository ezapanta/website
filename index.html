ENG 313 Projects

A collection of technical writing projects completed Spring 2017.

User Test Report: based on a test conducted for building a lego seaplane using a custom set of instructions. Included in these instructions are text and visual aids to help assemble the intended product. Thee instructions were split into three sections for simpler assembly.

Feasibility Study: Intended to measure if stress affects a large amount of Oxford, OH, with the goal of producing the stress management solution for citizens of the town. Polling, research, and assertions about stress are used.

Proposal: Meant to introduce the nonprofit Stress Management for the Masses. The company is aimed to help residents of all ages in Oxford find smart ways to deal with stress in their lives. Budgeting, scheduling, and benefits are explored.
